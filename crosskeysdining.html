About Us

Since our opening in 1998, we have strived to put the customer first. We have renovated numerous times since '98, as we look to make our ambiance also new and inviting to our patrons across the valley! We work with many different farmers and local suppliers, ensuring everything from crisp vegetables to fresh meats and cheeses. Come check us out today, you won't regret it!

Cross Keys Dining was started in 1998 by David Browne. He saw the beauty of the Shenandoah area, and knew that there was an opportunity to bring some of the best cuisine available to accumpany such a beautiful part of Virginia. Since its conception, Cross Keys Dining has been commited to serving the valley, with a great atmosphere and even better food!
